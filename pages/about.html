🧠 What is AI Face Analyzer?

AI Face Analyzer is a free web application by Tynixor that uses advanced AI and real face recognition technology to deliver engaging personality readings, beauty scoring, age detection, face symmetry analysis, future predictions, and much more.

All AI processing runs directly inside your browser. Your photos are never sent to any server — they stay 100% on your device. We built this platform with privacy as a non-negotiable foundation.
