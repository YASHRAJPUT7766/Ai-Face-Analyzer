2. Information We Collect

We want to be fully transparent about the data we collect. Here is a complete breakdown:

2.1 Images You Upload

When you use any analysis feature, your photo is processed primarily within your browser using on-device AI models. In some cases, a compressed version of your image may be temporarily sent to third-party face detection APIs (such as Face++ or similar services) solely to generate your results.

📷 Image Retention: Images are not permanently stored on our servers. Any temporary server-side processing is done over HTTPS and images are discarded immediately after results are generated.

2.3 Account Information (Google Sign-In)

If you choose to sign in using Google, we collect your Google display name, email address, and profile picture URL via Firebase Authentication. This is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Assign and persist your credit balance across devices
  • Display your name on the Leaderboard (if you rank)
  • Restore your analysis streak and history
  • Send important account notifications (if applicable)

2.4 Usage & Analysis Data

We collect anonymized data about how you interact with our tools, including:

  • Type of analysis performed (face, age, symmetry, compare, future)
  • Analysis scores and result categories
  • Credits consumed per session
  • Daily streak status and spin wheel results

2.5 Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser cookies and localStorage to:

  • 🍪 Remember your credit balance and usage history
  • 🔥 Track your daily analysis streak and last visit date
  • 🎰 Store spin wheel eligibility and last spin timestamp
  • ✅ Remember your consent acceptance
  • ⚙️ Save your settings preferences (theme, language, etc.)
  • 🔑 Maintain your login session via Firebase Auth tokens
  • 📍 Cache geolocation data (country/city) to avoid repeated API calls

You can clear all local data at any time via your browser settings or through Settings → Wipe All Data.

2.6 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may independently collect data:

  • Google AdSense — Displays ads on our website. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymized usage statistics.
  • Firebase Authentication — Manages Google Sign-In. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database — Stores leaderboard data, credit history, and anonymized visitor analytics.
  • Cloudinary — Used for temporary image storage in certain features. Images are stored securely and not shared with third parties.
  • Face++ / FaceAPI — Used for face detection and age estimation. Images sent to these APIs are subject to their privacy policies.
  • ipapi.co — Used for geolocation (country/city) based on your IP address.
