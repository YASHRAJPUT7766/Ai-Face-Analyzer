❓ Missions FAQ

What are AI Face Analyzer missions?

Missions are daily and weekly challenges — analyze faces, try features, build streaks — to earn XP, level up from Rookie to Legend, and win free credits. New missions refresh every day!

How do I earn XP and level up?

Earn XP by: analyzing faces (+80 XP each), keeping streaks (+40 XP daily), completing missions (up to +500 XP), and unlocking achievements. Level up through 8 ranks — Rookie → Explorer → Analyst → Expert → Master → Legend → Mythic → GOD TIER!

When do missions reset?

Daily missions and the daily challenge reset at midnight every day. Weekly missions reset every Monday morning. Complete them all before they expire!
