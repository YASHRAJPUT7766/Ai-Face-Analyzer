🤖 Technology

How Our AI Detects Your Age — Step by Step

Built on face-api.js deep learning. Here's exactly what happens the moment you click "Detect My Age".

01
📸
Photo Input & Face Verification
The AI runs a TinyFaceDetector pass first — scanning for a human face with a confidence score. If no face found (score < 40%), we reject the photo and ask for a better one. 99% detection accuracy on clear front-facing photos. No face = no fake age shown.
TinyFaceDetector · 320px input · Score threshold 0.4
02
🗺️
68-Point Facial Landmark Mapping
Once a face is confirmed, the AI maps 68 precise landmark coordinates — jaw contour (17 pts), eyebrows (10 pts), nose (9 pts), eyes (12 pts), mouth (20 pts). These form the structural skeleton for all age calculations.
faceLandmark68TinyNet · Sub-pixel precision
03
🔬
500+ Feature Extraction
From 68 landmarks, the engine computes 500+ derived features: inter-landmark distances, angular facial ratios, facial volume estimates, eye aperture width, nasolabial fold scoring, forehead height ratio, and more. Each feature is weighted by age-predictive power.
ageGenderNet · Multi-task CNN · Age + Gender simultaneous
04
🧬
Skin & Eye Zone Deep Analysis
Separately, the AI runs texture analysis on skin zones (forehead, cheeks, under-eyes) and eye structure analysis (lid droop angle, crow's feet zone, upper lid fullness). These give your "Skin Age" and "Eye Age" scores — which can differ from overall age.
Texture gradient scoring · Bilateral symmetry
05
🎂
Final Age + Timeline Generation
All 500+ feature scores feed the final age prediction. We output your detected age ± 1–2 years, then generate your −10 year past face and +10 year future face using canvas morphing effects. Full report in under 3 seconds.
Ensemble scoring · ±1–2yr margin · Canvas morphing
